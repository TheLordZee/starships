Welcome To StarShips At War!

StarShips At War is a fast paced strategy game as you and your opponent fight to gain resources and destroy each other planets.

Play!

  • Each ship costs a certain amount of materials to be built, indicated by the number underneath it's icon. The more expensive the ship, the more powerful it is.

  • Materials can be gained over time...

  • or by getting miners.

  • The enemy planet's health will go down as you attack it. You win when it disappears completely.

  • Good Luck!
